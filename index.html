My pet dragon Eve


My pet dragon Eve
Oh my sweet lovable pet dragon Eve
You are always there for me when I’m done
You cheer me up with your little tricks
You take my out for a joy ride with the clouds
Oh my pet dragon Eve
Every time I go to your cave you give me joy

Every time I play with you, you make me happy
You always bring a smile to my face
Oh my sweet lovable dragon Eve
You will always be my best friend/pet
I will never forget about you
I will never let no one take you
I will protect you my pet
You will never be tortured
You will never be threatened
My sweet innocent pet Eve
